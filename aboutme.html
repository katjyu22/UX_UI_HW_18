NICE TO MEET YOU!

My name is Katherine but you can call me Kathy. I am currently an aspiring UX/UI designer who is interested in design-centered problem solving.

I received my B.A. in psychology from UCR. Although I enjoyed learning about the human mind, I wanted to pursue a more creative avenue. After I graduated I took some graphic design classes at my local community college. Wanting to pursue design more seriously, my instructor recommended I look into UX/UI and that brought me to where I am today.

I love to be creative whether that is illustrating or solving problems. I feel encouraged to grow in an environment of authenticity and being challenged creatively.
